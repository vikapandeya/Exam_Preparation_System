💡 Study Tips
🎯

Set Daily Targets

Aim to cover at least 2–3 subjects per day. Consistency beats cramming every time for competitive exams.

🔁

Spaced Repetition

Revisit subjects after 1 day, 3 days, then 7 days. This technique dramatically improves long-term retention.

📝

Active Recall

After reading each section, close the page and write down what you remember. Tests understanding, not reading.

⏱️

Pomodoro Method

Study for 25 minutes, take a 5-minute break. After 4 cycles, take a 20-minute break. Proven to reduce fatigue.

🗺️

Start With Weaknesses

Open your least-visited subjects first when your energy is highest. Leave comfortable topics for later sessions.

Check Your Syllabus
