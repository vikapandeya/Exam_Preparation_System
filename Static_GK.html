📌 UN Security Council Permanent Members (P5): USA, UK, France, Russia, China — have veto power. Non-permanent: 10 members elected for 2-year terms. India's bid for permanent seat — supported by USA, UK, France, Russia. UN Secretary General — António Guterres (Portugal); elected 2017, re-elected 2021. UN founded 1945; 193 member states.
🌐 Key 2023–24 Updates:
• BRICS Expansion (Jan 2024): 5 new members added — Iran, UAE, Egypt, Ethiopia, Saudi Arabia. Original 5: Brazil, Russia, India, China, South Africa.
• NATO: 32 members; Sweden joined March 2024 (Finland joined 2023). HQ: Brussels.
• G20 India Presidency 2023: Theme — "Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam" (One Earth, One Family, One Future). Members: 19 countries + EU + African Union (AU added 2023).
• G7: USA, UK, Canada, France, Germany, Italy, Japan (+EU as observer).
• SCO: 9 members; India joined 2017. HQ Beijing.
• SAARC: 8 members (India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Nepal, Bhutan, Sri Lanka, Maldives, Afghanistan). HQ Kathmandu. Inactive since 2016 summit.
• ASEAN: 10 members. Secretariat: Jakarta, Indonesia.
• ILO (1919): Oldest UN specialised agency; Geneva; labour standards.
